Our Story

Committed to making sustainable packaging accessible for every food business in Singapore

Our Mission

Between CCAs, project meetings and grabbing quick dinners around the same stretch of restaurants near school, we kept noticing how much disposable packaging piled up every evening. Most of the places we visited wanted to adopt greener options, but the owners told us the same thing each time. Sustainable packaging cost more, delivery times were unreliable and switching over created risks they could not take on easily.

We were still students, but the pattern was too clear to ignore. These were neighbourhood restaurants that shaped our daily lives, and it felt wrong that wanting to be environmentally responsible came with so many hurdles. So we started small. We asked questions, compared materials, spoke with suppliers and tried to understand what would make sustainable packaging realistic instead of idealistic. That became the starting point for Renvia. Our work has always been built around one belief that guides every decision we make: Because it doesn't need to cost the Earth.
