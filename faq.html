Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our sustainable packaging solutions

General Questions

Our packaging is made from renewable, plant-based materials like bamboo, sugarcane fiber, and corn starch. They are 100% compostable and biodegradable, breaking down completely within 90 days under commercial composting conditions, unlike conventional plastics that can take decades to decompose.

While our sustainable packaging may have a slightly higher upfront cost compared to some conventional plastics, many of our clients find they save money in the long run through reduced waste disposal fees, improved brand image, and customer loyalty. We also offer bulk discounts to help make our solutions more affordable.

Product Questions

Most of our food containers are microwave-safe for up to 2-3 minutes at medium power. However, we recommend removing lids before microwaving as some condensation may form. Always check the specific product specifications for details.

Our containers are designed with a special PLA lining that makes them resistant to oils and sauces for typical meal durations (2-3 hours). For very oily foods or longer durations, we recommend our sugarcane-based containers which have superior oil resistance.

Absolutely! We offer custom printing with your logo, branding, or other designs. Minimum order quantities apply for customized products. Our design team can help you create packaging that aligns with your brand identity.

Ordering & Delivery

Our standard MOQ is 500 units per product, but we can accommodate smaller trial orders for first-time customers. For custom printed items, the MOQ is typically 1,000 units.

For in-stock items, we typically deliver within 3-5 business days across Singapore. Rush delivery (next day) is available for an additional fee. Lead times for custom orders vary based on complexity but average 2-3 weeks.

Yes, we offer tiered pricing discounts for larger orders. Discounts start at 5% for orders over 5,000 units and can go up to 15% for orders exceeding 20,000 units. Contact our sales team for a customized quote based on your specific needs.

Sustainability

Our products can be disposed of in commercial composting facilities where available. In Singapore, they can go into general waste where they will break down much faster than plastic. We provide educational materials you can share with your customers about proper disposal.

Yes, all our compostable products meet international standards (ASTM D6400 and EN13432) for compostability. We can provide certification documents upon request.

Our lifecycle analysis shows that our packaging generates 60-70% fewer greenhouse gas emissions compared to equivalent petroleum-based plastics. The plants used to make our materials also absorb CO2 as they grow, making our products carbon neutral over their lifecycle.
